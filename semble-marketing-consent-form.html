Your Rights & Withdrawal of Consent

You have the right to:

  • Withdraw consent at any time by emailing marketing@semble.io

  • Review content before publication (where feasible)
  • Access a copy of this consent form at any time

What Happens When You Withdraw Consent

If you withdraw consent, Semble Marketing will take the following actions:

On owned channels (full control):

  • Remove or replace website pages, testimonials, and images
  • Delete or unpublish social media posts where feasible
  • Remove from future email campaigns and sales collateral
  • Stop using in webinars and presentations going forward

On paid advertising campaigns:

  • Stop or pause ads immediately
  • Remove creatives from active ad sets

On third-party or reshared content (limited control):

  • We will delete our original posts
  • However, we may not be able to immediately erase reposts, screenshots, press copies, or cached versions created by third parties
  • We will document all actions taken and provide you with a summary

Timeline: We will action removal requests within 30 days and provide confirmation once complete.

Important: Withdrawing consent will not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before withdrawal.
